How to Buy Raws

Support the original authors by purchasing official raws! Here's a step-by-step guide for each store. You don't need to read Japanese — just follow the steps.

1

step 01

Create a Bookwalker account

Go to bookwalker.jp and click 新規登録 (New Registration) in the top right. Enter your email and create a password. You'll get a confirmation email — click the link inside to verify.
tip — Bookwalker JP is separate from Bookwalker Global. Make sure you're on bookwalker.jp not bookwalker.com.tw or global.bookwalker.jp.
2

step 02

Add coins to your account

Bookwalker uses a coin system. Go to コイン購入 (Buy Coins) and choose an amount. You can pay with a credit card — Visa and Mastercard from overseas work fine. 100 coins = ¥100 roughly.
tip — Bookwalker often runs coin-back campaigns where you get bonus coins on purchases. Check the top banner before buying!
3

step 03

Search for your manga

Use the search bar at the top. Search by the Japanese title or author name. You can also paste the title from our project pages directly. Click the result to go to the manga's page.
tip — If you can't find it by title, try searching the author's name — their full catalog will show up.
4

step 04

Purchase the volume

On the manga page, click カートに入れる (Add to Cart) or 今すぐ購入 (Buy Now). Select which volume you want. Confirm the purchase — your coins will be deducted and the book will appear in your library instantly.
5

step 05

Read on the Bookwalker app

Download the BOOK☆WALKER app on your phone or tablet, or read in browser. Log in with the same account and your purchases will be there. The app works offline once downloaded!
tip — Bookwalker has a free trial chapter for most manga — great for checking if you like the art style before buying.
step 1 / 5

→ Visit bookwalker.jp

1

step 01

Create a Rakuten account

Go to books.rakuten.co.jp and click 新規会員登録 (New Member Registration). You'll need a valid email. Rakuten is one of Japan's biggest retailers so the catalog is huge.
tip — Rakuten also has Kobo — their e-reader platform. If you own a Kobo device, purchases sync automatically!
2

step 02

Register your payment method

Go to お支払い方法の設定 (Payment Settings) and add your credit card. Rakuten accepts international Visa and Mastercard. You can also use 楽天ポイント (Rakuten Points) if you have any from previous purchases.
3

step 03

Search for your manga

Search by Japanese title or author in the top search bar. Filter by コミック・ラノベ・BL to narrow results. Rakuten carries both physical and digital — look for 電子書籍 listings for instant delivery.
4

step 04

Purchase the digital version

Click on the digital listing (marked 電子書籍) and click 今すぐ購入 (Buy Now). Confirm your payment method and complete the purchase. You'll earn Rakuten Points on every purchase!
tip — Rakuten runs frequent point campaigns — sometimes 5x or 10x points on manga. Check the campaign banner before buying!
5

step 05

Read on Rakuten Kobo

Download the Rakuten Kobo app or read in browser. Your purchases appear in your library immediately. The Kobo app is available on iOS, Android, and desktop — and works offline too.
