FAQ

Because our members' schedules and ages vary, we cannot maintain a fixed or specific release schedule.

Our main focus is BL (Boys’ Love) manga. Depending on Kakui’s mood, our projects range from sweet, fluffy stories to heartbreaking “sobbing” dramas and occasionally even full-on horror.

We're not currently taking requests. We only work on series we've personally chosen to pick up. If that changes, we'll announce it.

Please don't repost without credit. If you want to share our work, link back to this site. Aggregator sites are fine as long as they credit Color Dodge.
